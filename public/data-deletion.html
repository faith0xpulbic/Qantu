Requesting deletion of your data

If you have messaged a business that uses Qantu on WhatsApp or Instagram, you can request that we delete the data we hold about you — including your name, contact details, and message history — at any time.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to hello@qantu.app with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include the phone number or Instagram username you used to message the business, so we can locate your records
  3. We will confirm your request and remove your data from our systems within 30 days
Deleting your data will remove your conversation history from Qantu's systems. It does not delete messages stored directly within WhatsApp or Instagram themselves — those are governed by Meta's own data policies.

What gets deleted

  • Your name and contact information as stored in our system
  • The full content of conversations associated with your contact details
  • Any summaries or notes generated from your conversations

Contact

For any questions about this process, reach us at hello@qantu.app.
