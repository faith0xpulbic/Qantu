For the review team

How to test this app

Qantu has no website login or dashboard to click through. All functionality lives inside a live Instagram conversation. Here's exactly how to see it work:

  1. Open Instagram and send a Direct Message to @favautomationstudio
  2. Send any message — for example: "Hi, do you have this in stock?"
  3. Qantu's assistant replies automatically within a few seconds, using the instagram_business_manage_messages permission to read and respond to the message
  4. Continue the conversation naturally — ask a follow-up question, or say something like "I've made the payment" to see how Qantu recognizes when a human business owner needs to step in

Privacy Policy

Last updated — July 2026

Who we are

Qantu ("we", "us", "our") provides an AI-assisted messaging service that helps businesses respond to customer inquiries on WhatsApp and Instagram.

What information we collect

When you message a business using our service, we may collect and process:

  • Your name and phone number or Instagram username
  • The content of messages you send to the connected business account
  • Images or attachments sent as part of the conversation
  • Timestamps and metadata related to your messages

How we use this information

We use this information solely to respond to your messages on behalf of the business you contacted, generate summaries for the business owner when human review is needed, and maintain conversation history so responses stay relevant and accurate. We do not sell your information, and we do not use your messages for advertising.

Data storage & retention

Message data is stored securely and is only accessible to the business you messaged and our system for the purpose of providing this service. We retain conversation data for as long as necessary to provide the service, or until the business or user requests deletion.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by contacting the business you messaged directly, or by reaching us using the details below.

Third-party services

Our service uses the WhatsApp Business Platform and Instagram Messaging API, both provided by Meta, to send and receive messages. Your use of WhatsApp or Instagram is also subject to Meta's own privacy policy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@qantu.app.
